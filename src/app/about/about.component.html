Our Story

TravelPlanner was born from a simple frustration: planning trips was too complicated and time-consuming. After hours spent researching destinations, comparing options, and creating itineraries, our founders realized there had to be a better way.

In 2020, we set out to build the travel planning tool we wished existed. We combined our expertise in technology, design, and travel to create a platform that makes discovering and planning trips as exciting as the journey itself.

Today, TravelPlanner helps thousands of travelers discover amazing destinations and create personalized itineraries. But we're just getting started on our mission to make travel planning effortless and inspiring for everyone.
